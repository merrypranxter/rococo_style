## Family 1: Rocaille (Shell and Scroll)

The foundational Rococo ornament vocabulary. Derived from shells, rocks, and coral forms, combined into free-form, asymmetrical, often pierced motifs.

| ID | Name | Description | Typical Placement |
|---|---|---|---|
| `rocaille_shell_central` | Central shell crest | Stylized scallop shell as the dominant crest of a frame or mirror | Mirror top, overdoor, panel crest |
| `rocaille_scatter_edge` | Edge shell fragments | Small shell/rock fragments at frame corners or along moldings | Frame corners, dado rail |
| `rocaille_grotto` | Grotto cluster | Rock, shell, and coral combined into cave-like mass | Garden grottoes, fountain bases, wallpaper |
| `rocaille_cartouche` | Rocaille cartouche | Asymmetric scroll frame, often enclosing a title, crest, or small scene | Wall panels, book frontispieces, map cartouches |
| `scroll_C` | C-scroll | Single open C-shaped curve used as building block of larger ornament | Everywhere — chair rails, mirror sides, furniture aprons |
| `scroll_S` | S-scroll | Double reverse curve; spine of most Rococo ornamental flourishes | Frame borders, furniture legs, stucco bands |
| `scroll_interlaced` | Interlaced scroll | Two or more scrolls intertwined, often with foliage threading through | Overdoors, ceiling borders, textile patterns |

## Family 2: Flora and Vegetation

Floral motifs are ubiquitous in Rococo across painting, textiles, furniture carving, and porcelain.

| ID | Name | Description | Typical Placement |
|---|---|---|---|
| `motif_flower_rose` | Rose bouquet | Lush, open roses, often in loose clusters with leaves | Upholstery, dress fabric, garland, porcelain |
| `motif_flower_peony` | Peony cluster | Full, billowing peony heads; associated with Chinoiserie inflections | Wallpaper, textile, overdoor panel |
| `motif_flower_tulip` | Tulip spray | Upright tulips, sometimes stylized; links to Dutch influences | Textile, ceramic |
| `motif_leaf_acanthus` | Acanthus scroll | Stylized acanthus leaf wrapping around shells and frames | Cornice, furniture legs, stucco |
| `motif_garland_swag` | Floral garland swag | Long festoon of flowers and leaves hung in an arc | Overdoors, ceiling borders, tapestry headers |
| `motif_wreath_laurel` | Laurel wreath | Oval wreath of laurel, often enclosing portraits or emblems | Portrait ovals, medal-like panels |
| `motif_vine_tendril` | Trailing vine tendril | Thin spiraling stems threading through ornament bands | Textile borders, scroll infill |
